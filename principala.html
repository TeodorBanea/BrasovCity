About Brasov

Facts:

  • Location: Central Romania (Brasov County)
  • Size: 28.5 sq. miles (74 sq. km)
  • Elevation: 2,133 ft. (650 meters)
  • Population: 275,000
  • Inhabited since: 100 BC
  • First documented: 1234 AD (Corona)
