Complex Ambient

Ideally located in the centre of Brasov, a few minutes away from the historic monuments, elegant bars and glamorous shops, this hotel is a reference point for luxury accommodation. Enjoy superb views of the city, perfect service and culinary delights in the hotel's restaurant. Utmost attention is paid to every single detail, ensuring that you spend an unforgettable stay at the Ambient hotel. Don't miss to explore the picturesque Old Town of Brasov with its mansions of the late Middle Ages and elegant 19th-century buildings. Guests of this property benefit from discounts and preferential prices to several restaurants, pubs, as well as cultural and touristic sites. The property offers a free shuttle to Casino Vesuvius, reachable in approximately 14 minutes by car. This is our guests' favourite part of Brasov, according to independent reviews. Aro Pallace

Overlooking the Tampa Mountain and the medieval historic quarter of Brasov, the traditional Hotel Aro Palace faces the Central Park. It features spacious and luxurious accommodation and free internet access. The rooms have air conditioning, cable TV and bathrobes. They are designed in warm colours and have carpeted floors. Meal info: Guests can sample Romanian and European cuisine at the hotel's restaurant and enjoy the views over Brasov's Citadel from the Belvedere Club, which is on Hotel Aro Palace's top floor. The hotel's Night Bar is decorated with marble, wood and Art Deco elements. The chandeliers and large mirrors create a special atmosphere where guests can sip their favorite beverages. Aro Palace Spa & Wellness Centre offers various treatments and beauty procedures. It features a swimming pool, saunas, massage areas and a gym. Kronwell Complex

Kronwell Brasov Hotel is a contemporary 4-star superior property offering luxury accommodation in the heart of Brasov. Highlights include an indoor swimming pool, a sauna, a steam room and a good location near public transport links. Brasov is a historic city with some fine architecture including medieval alleys, Saxon citadels and fortified churches. Nearby places of interest include the Black Church, Mount Tampa and the First Romanian School Museum. Local bus services provide fast and easy access to other popular Brasov destinations. The hotel is housed in a contemporary high-rise building. Facilities include an indoor swimming pool, a sauna and a steam room. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the premises. Business rooms are 34sqm and decorated in a modern style with warm tones. Amenities include air-conditioning, a flat-screen satellite TV, a mini-bar, a safe and a work desk. The en-suite bathroom features a bathtub and a separate walk-in shower. Guests can also enjoy a range of drinks at the hotel bar. A buffet breakfast is provided every morning. Free parking is possible onsite. The hotel has a 24-hour reception.
